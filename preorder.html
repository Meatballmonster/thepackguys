★ How It Works ★

Five steps. No surprises.

  1. Pick your tier. Same posted pricing as the catalog — 1K / 5K / 25K / 50K cases. Per-unit locks at today's rate.
  2. Reserve with 20% deposit. Pay via Stripe (card, ACH, USDC). Your deposit is held against the final invoice — not a separate fee.
  3. Container lands in LA on the ETA (July 2026). You get an email the day it clears customs at the Port of Los Angeles.
  4. Your order ships within 5 business days of container arrival in queue order (first deposit, first ship). Balance invoice goes out the same day.
  5. If container slips > 14 days from quoted ETA, you can request a full deposit refund — no questions, no fee.
Honest reality check: Pack Guys' first container is in transit. We are not yet shipping. Your reservation is a real commitment in both directions — you lock the tier price, we lock your queue position. The 14-day grace clause exists so you aren't stuck holding deposit cash if ocean freight delays us.
