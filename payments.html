✓ Payments · all posted

Four ways to pay.

Net-30 unlocks after your second invoice clears. No surprises, no merchant-account theater. ACH and crypto buyers shouldn't subsidize card users — so the card fee is passed through, not baked in.

ACH / Wire — best path

Direct bank transfer, same-day clearing for orders before 1pm PT. 2% off order #1 when paid via ACH within 48 hours of invoice. No fees.

Check

Order ships once the check clears (typically 5–7 business days from postmark). For a faster turn, use ACH or wire. Best for sample kits and orders under $1,500.

Card — Visa / MC / Amex / Discover

Processed via Stripe (cannabis-accessory eligible). A 3.5% surcharge is passed through on card transactions to cover processor fees — we'd rather show it than hide it in base prices.

Crypto — USDC / BTC / ETH

USDC earns a 1.5% prompt-pay discount on every invoice. Send to the wallet on your invoice; the order pulls on confirmation.

Net-30 terms

Unlocked after your second paid invoice clears. Standard Net-30 from invoice date. Late beyond Net-45 returns the account to prepay. Split payment (deposit by card, balance by ACH) is fine on first orders — note it on your PO.
