How To Pay.

Four ways to pay, all posted. Net-30 unlocks after your second invoice clears. No surprises, no merchant-account theater.

★ Four Methods ★

Pick Your Lane.

★ Method 01 · Preferred
ACH / Wire.
Direct bank transfer. Same-day clearing for orders before 1 PM PT. 2% discount on order #1 when paid via ACH within 48 hours of invoice.

Bank details emailed with invoice. Reference your invoice # on the wire memo.
★ Method 02 · Standard
Check.
Mail to: The Pack Guys LLC, 915 Mateo St #312, Los Angeles, CA 90021.

Order ships once check clears (typically 5–7 business days from postmark). For faster turn, use ACH or wire instead.
★ Method 03 · Convenient
Credit / Debit Card.
Visa, MC, Amex, Discover. Processed via Stripe (cannabis-accessory eligible). 3.5% surcharge passed through on card transactions to cover processor fees.

Best for sample kits and orders under $1,500.
★ Method 04 · Earned
Net-30 Terms.
Unlocked after your second paid invoice clears. Standard Net-30 from invoice date. Late beyond Net-45 = back to prepay.

Application required (1-page credit refs). Submit on the wholesale form.

★ FAQ ★

Common Questions.

Do you take crypto?
Not yet. ACH/wire is cleaner for both sides. We'll revisit when stablecoin rails settle.
Why a card surcharge?
Cannabis-accessory processors charge 3–4% to merchants. We pass it through instead of pricing it into everyone's tube cost. ACH/wire customers shouldn't subsidize card users.
What if I'm late on Net-30?
Day 31–45: friendly reminder. Day 46: back to prepay only. Day 60: 1.5%/month late fee accrues. We don't sell debt — we just stop extending terms.
Can I split payment methods?
Yes — deposit by card, balance by ACH is common on first orders. Note the split on your PO or in the order notes.
Do you take PO numbers?
Yes. Include your PO # at checkout or on your wholesale application. We'll reference it on the invoice + packing list.
