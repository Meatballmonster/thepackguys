HOW TO PAY

five ways to settle.

Card, ACH, wire, USDC stablecoin, or crypto (Bitcoin and Ethereum). Whichever fits your treasury — we settle in minutes for crypto, days for traditional banking.

RECOMMENDED FOR WHOLESALE
USDC ·

usdc stablecoin.

USD-pegged digital dollar. No volatility, no banking delays, settles in minutes via Coinbase Commerce.

  • Fee: ~1.0% (vs 2.9% card)
  • Settlement: 5-10 minutes
  • Volatility: none (1:1 USD)
  • Networks supported: Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, Base
  • Best for: 5K+ orders where ~$5-30 fee savings adds up
FASTEST FIAT
CARD

credit or debit card.

Stripe-processed. We send an invoice link, you pay with any major card. Most common for first-time buyers.

  • Fee: 2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe rate)
  • Settlement: instant
  • Best for: first orders, sample cases, small repeat orders
LOWEST FEE FIAT
ACH

ach or wire transfer.

Bank-to-bank. We send wiring instructions on invoice; you initiate from your business banking.

  • Fee: $0 (ACH) or $25 (wire)
  • Settlement: 1-3 business days (ACH), same-day (wire)
  • Best for: 25K+ orders, established wholesale accounts on net-30
OPERATOR-FRIENDLY
BTC · ETH

bitcoin or ethereum.

Native crypto via Coinbase Commerce. Rate locks for 15 minutes at checkout. We auto-convert to USD on receipt.

  • Fee: ~1.0% + network gas
  • Settlement: 10-60 minutes (depending on network confirmations)
  • Volatility risk: handled by 15-min rate lock
  • Best for: operators with crypto treasury holdings who want to deploy non-fiat capital

why we built crypto rails.

B2B cannabis runs on cash because banks won't touch the category. Crypto rails settle in minutes, don't get debanked, and let you deploy treasury without converting to dollars first. We're not crypto-maxis — we just think operators deserve more options than wire or card.
