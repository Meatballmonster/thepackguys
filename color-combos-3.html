★ Round-table 3 — what to test

Eldridge: "Coral is right. Test the TEAL — that's where the variation lives."
Rashid: "Push saturation higher. The whole point of distinct palette is reading at 30ft."
Eames: "Three colors is a floor. Teal + coral + mustard creates a triadic system."
Albers: "Same coral on a deeper teal vs brighter teal reads as different hue. Pick the RELATIONSHIP."
Panton: "Restraint is overrated. Saturate the teal. Saturate the accent."
Jongerius: "Add a grounding color — black, sage, brown — so palette has weight, doesn't float."
