Pre-order terms.

Plain English. Short version: 20% deposit, 14-day delay grace, full refund if we slip.

1. what a pre-order reservation is

A pre-order reservation is a commitment between you (the buyer) and The Pack Guys LLC (Pack Guys). You commit to purchase a specific tier quantity of Pack Guys 116mm child-resistant pre-roll tubes from our first-batch inventory. Pack Guys commits to ship that quantity at the locked tier price as soon as our first container clears customs and is processed for distribution at our LA Arts District warehouse.

2. deposit

20% of your tier total is due at the time of reservation. The deposit is held against your final invoice, not collected as a separate fee. When your order ships, the balance invoice (80% of tier total + any applicable shipping) is due Net-30 or pay-on-ship, your choice.

3. estimated ship date

The current quoted ETA is July 2026. This reflects our container's transit, customs clearance, and warehouse processing schedule. We will email you the day the container clears customs at the Port of Los Angeles and again the day your specific order ships.

4. queue order

Pre-order fulfillment is first-deposit, first-ship. The order in which paid deposits are received determines shipping queue position. Each order ships within 5 business days of container arrival, in queue order.

5. delay grace clause

If the container slips more than 14 calendar days past the quoted ETA, you may request a full deposit refund — no questions, no fee, no cancellation friction. Email sales@thepackguys.com with your reservation number to request the refund. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 business days.

If the container arrives within the 14-day grace window, your reservation remains active and your tier price stays locked. We may, at our discretion, extend the grace window if customs delays exceed our forecast.

6. cancellation

You may cancel your reservation at any time before your specific order ships. If you cancel before the container has cleared customs, your deposit is fully refunded. After the container clears customs but before your order ships, your deposit is fully refundable minus a 5% processing fee (covers our queue management and payment processor charges). After your order ships, standard returns policy applies.

7. tier price lock

The tier price quoted at the time of your reservation is locked for first-batch fulfillment and remains valid for the duration of your reservation. If our posted tier prices change before your order ships, you pay the lower of (a) your locked reservation price, or (b) current posted price. We will not charge you more than your locked price.

8. spec, cert, color

Your reservation covers Pack Guys 116mm ASTM D3475 / 16 CFR § 1700.20 certified pre-roll tubes. Color preference selected at reservation is non-binding; final color allocation depends on container stock and queue position. If your preferred color is unavailable at ship time, we will contact you to confirm substitution or split allocation before shipping.

9. net-30 eligibility

Your first-batch order (the one against this reservation) ships against the balance invoice. Net-30 terms unlock for your second paid order, per our standard wholesale terms.

10. payment processor + chargebacks

Reservations are processed via Stripe Payment Links (card, ACH, USDC). Stripe's standard terms apply. Filing a payment-processor chargeback before invoking the 14-day grace clause (Section 5) waives the grace-clause refund and is governed by Stripe's standard dispute process. We strongly prefer you email us first — refunds are faster and cleaner that way.

11. force majeure

Pack Guys is not liable for container delays caused by events outside our reasonable control, including (without limitation) port strikes, customs holds, weather events, war, or pandemic. In a force-majeure delay scenario, the 14-day grace clause still applies: you can still request a full deposit refund after 14 days.

12. governing law + jurisdiction

These terms are governed by the laws of the State of California. Any dispute arising from a pre-order reservation will be resolved in Los Angeles County, California.

13. questions

Email sales@thepackguys.com. We respond within one business day, Mon-Fri.
