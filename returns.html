★ Returns Policy ★

Returns & RMAs.

30 days from delivery. Damaged or defective gets credited or reshipped. Buyer's remorse returns accepted at restocking + freight.

The Short Version

  • Damaged in transit: 100% credited or reshipped. Photograph the carton + contents within 48 hours.
  • Defective product (CR fail, cracked, off-spec): 100% credited or reshipped. No restocking fee.
  • Wrong color/quantity shipped (our error): 100% credited or corrected. We pay return freight.
  • Changed your mind (buyer's remorse): 15% restocking fee + you pay return freight. Must be sealed, unopened cases.
  • Custom-printed orders: Non-returnable except for defects. By definition you ordered something only you can use.

RMA Process

  1. Email help@thepackguys.com within 30 days of delivery. Include your order #, photos if applicable, and the issue.
  2. We respond within 1 business day with an RMA # and instructions.
  3. For damaged/defective: don't ship back yet — we may just credit you. For wrong-ship or buyer's remorse: we'll email a return label or address.
  4. Pack the cases the same way they arrived. Tape the RMA # to the outside.
  5. Once received, credit or replacement processes in 3–5 business days.

Damaged Shipment Documentation

For carrier-damaged shipments, we need photos to file the claim:

  • One photo of the outer carton showing the damage (do this before opening further).
  • One photo of the shipping label clearly visible.
  • One photo of the damaged tubes inside.
  • Brief description of what's wrong.

The carrier requires this for any claim above $100. We file with them; you don't need to talk to UPS/FedEx.

What Counts As Defective

A tube is defective if:

  • The child-resistant mechanism fails to operate per spec (push-and-turn doesn't engage).
  • The cap doesn't seat properly when closed.
  • The body is cracked, deformed, or off-color from spec.
  • The tube is contaminated or unclean from manufacture.

If you have a 1,000-unit case and 5 tubes are bad, we'll credit you for those 5. If 50+ tubes are bad, we credit the case and want it back so we can QC it.

Credits vs Refunds

Credits apply to your next order automatically — no action needed, just place the order and we'll net it against the credit. Refunds to original payment method are available on request; allow 5–7 business days for the bank to post.

Questions

Email help@thepackguys.com. We respond within 24 hours Mon–Fri.
