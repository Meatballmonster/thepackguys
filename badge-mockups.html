★ Why TPG > PG

PG reads as Procter & Gamble (the consumer-goods megacorp). 90% of B2B buyers will see "PG" on a packaging brand and pattern-match to the household-products giant. Confusing + minor legal-overlap risk.

TPG = "The Pack Guys" exactly. Three letters maps to the domain (thepackguys.com). Mono treatment makes it read like an SKU prefix or a freight handle. TPG Inc. exists (private equity), but they're so far from cannabis B2B that buyer confusion is negligible.
