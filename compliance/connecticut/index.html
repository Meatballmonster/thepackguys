Resources · State compliance

Connecticut pre-roll tube compliance.

This is the operator-side summary of what Connecticut's cannabis regulator requires for pre-roll tube packaging in 2026. Statute references are the controlling law; this guide cites them but isn't a substitute for legal counsel.

what CT requires for pre-roll tubes

  1. Public Act 21-1 established adult-use cannabis in Connecticut; retail sales began January 2023.
  2. Regs. Conn. State Agencies § 21a-422 requires child-resistant packaging for adult-use cannabis products including pre-rolls.
  3. DCP-prescribed warning copy + THC content + universal symbol on every retail label.
  4. Opaque packaging required at the retail point of sale.
  5. CR cert documentation (lab PDF, 16 CFR § 1700.20 protocol) on file with the licensee.
  6. Tamper-evident seal at point of sale.

which Pack Guys tubes meet CT rules

All five Pack Guys 116mm SKUs — black, clear, white, smoke, silver — are tested and certified to ASTM D3475 / 16 CFR § 1700.20, which is the controlling federal standard CT incorporates by reference. Each comes with a lab test PDF deliverable within 24 hours of request.

For CT retail markets that require opaque finishes, the black / white / silver / smoke options apply. Clear tubes are acceptable for B2B / co-pack shipments where the retailer applies the final opaque label or transfer.
