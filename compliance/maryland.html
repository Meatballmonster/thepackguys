what MD requires for pre-roll tubes

  1. HB 556 (2023) established adult-use cannabis in Maryland; retail sales began July 1, 2023.
  2. COMAR 14.17 requires child-resistant packaging for adult-use cannabis including pre-rolls.
  3. MCA-prescribed warning copy + THC content + Maryland cannabis symbol on every retail label.
  4. Opaque packaging required at the retail point of sale.
  5. CR cert documentation (lab PDF, 16 CFR § 1700.20 protocol) on file with the licensee.
  6. Tamper-evident seal at point of sale.

which Pack Guys tubes meet MD rules

All five Pack Guys 116mm SKUs — black, clear, white, smoke, silver — are tested and certified to ASTM D3475 / 16 CFR § 1700.20, which is the controlling federal standard MD incorporates by reference. Each comes with a lab test PDF deliverable within 24 hours of request.
