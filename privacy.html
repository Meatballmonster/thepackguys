PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy policy.

How we handle your information. Short version: we collect only what we need to ship you tubes, we do not sell it, and we delete it when we no longer need it.

what we collect

When you fill out a wholesale account application or sample order form, we collect: business name, contact name, email, phone, shipping address, and (for wholesale) trade references. When you browse, we collect basic analytics — page views, clicks, country-level location — via Google Analytics 4. We do not collect or store payment card numbers; those are handled by Stripe directly.

what we do with it

  • Wholesale + sample applications: contact you about your order, ship the product, send invoices, and follow up if anything changes
  • Analytics: understand which pages help buyers and improve the site
  • Marketing: if you opt in, occasional updates about new products or stock — never more than once a month, always one-click unsubscribe

what we do not do

We do not sell your information. We do not share it with third-party marketers. We do not use it to retarget you across the web (we run a Meta Pixel for ad measurement only, when ad campaigns are active).

your rights

Under California CCPA and EU GDPR, you have the right to: know what we have, request a copy, ask us to delete it, and opt out of any data sale (we do not sell anyway). Email privacy@thepackguys.com with any such request and we respond within 30 days.

cookies

We use first-party cookies for session state (remembering you dismissed the promo banner) and Google Analytics cookies for traffic measurement. No third-party tracking beyond GA4 and an optional Meta Pixel for ad measurement during paid campaigns.

data retention

Wholesale account information: kept while you have an active account, deleted within 90 days of account closure. Sample order information: kept 24 months for fulfillment records, then deleted. Analytics data: 14 months in Google Analytics, auto-purged.
