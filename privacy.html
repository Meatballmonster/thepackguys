Privacy Policy.

What we collect, how we use it, what we don't do with it.

The Short Version

  • We collect what you tell us on order forms — name, brand, email, shipping address, phone.
  • We use it to ship your order, send invoices, and reply to your messages.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.
  • We use Google Analytics 4 with IP anonymization and Consent Mode v2 (denied by default).
  • You can request deletion of your data anytime at help@thepackguys.com.

1. Who We Are

The Pack Guys LLC, 915 Mateo St #312, Los Angeles, CA 90021 ("we", "us"). Reach us at help@thepackguys.com for any privacy questions.

2. What We Collect

Information you give us: name, business name, email, phone, shipping/billing address, order details, payment method (processed by Stripe — we don't store card numbers).

Information collected automatically: Google Analytics 4 cookies (anonymized IP, page views, referrer). Cloudflare may set security cookies to protect against bot traffic.

What we do NOT collect: social media tracking pixels, advertising IDs, cross-site behavioral profiles. We don't operate a Facebook Pixel or TikTok Pixel.

3. How We Use It

  • Ship your orders and provide tracking.
  • Send invoices, statements, and order confirmations.
  • Respond to your sales/support emails.
  • Analyze aggregate site usage (which pages, what times, broad geography) to improve the site.
  • Comply with applicable cannabis-accessory and tax laws.

4. Who We Share It With

We share data only with service providers necessary to run the business:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • UPS / FedEx / USPS — shipping carriers
  • Cloudflare — DNS, email routing, bot protection
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Google Analytics — aggregated traffic analysis
  • Our accountant — invoice and tax records

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it for advertising or marketing of third-party products.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use Google Consent Mode v2. By default, analytics_storage, ad_storage, ad_user_data, and ad_personalization are all set to denied. Functional and security cookies (cart state, anti-bot tokens) are essential and run without consent. We do not currently surface a cookie banner because nothing requiring consent fires until you opt in.

6. Data Retention

Order records: 7 years (IRS retention requirement). Email correspondence: 3 years. Analytics aggregates: 26 months (Google default). You can request deletion of personal data at any time, except records required by law.

7. Your Rights

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): You have the right to know what we've collected, request deletion, request correction, and opt out of "sales" (we don't sell, but the right exists). Email help@thepackguys.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): Same rights plus data portability. Same email path.

We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

8. Security

HTTPS everywhere. Cloudflare in front of all traffic. Payment data tokenized via Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1). Internal access to customer records is limited to ownership and accounting. No system is 100% secure; we use reasonable measures and notify you of any breach affecting your data per applicable law.

9. Children

This site sells cannabis-accessory products to licensed businesses and B2B customers. It is not directed to anyone under 21. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 21.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the "Last Updated" date and email customers with active orders. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, complaints: help@thepackguys.com.
