## 3. FAQ (25 questions)

**Are these CR-certified?**
Yes. Every SKU is tested to 16 CFR 1700 / ASTM D3475. The test report PDF is at /cert — download it any time, share it with your compliance team.

**What's the MOQ?**
For stock SKUs (black, clear, white, smoke, black-with-silver-cap): no minimum. You can buy a single 25-pack. Cases are sold in 1,000-unit increments. Custom-printed orders start at 1,000 units. Custom-color or custom-print runs from our factory start at 10,000 (print) or 25,000 (color).

**When will my order ship?**
Stock orders placed before 1pm Pacific ship the same business day. After 1pm or weekend → next business day. Custom-labeled orders ship in 5–7 business days. Custom factory runs ship in 4–6 weeks.

**Where do you ship from?**
All stock orders ship from our Los Angeles warehouse. Custom factory runs ship direct from our partner factory in China with duty + freight rolled into your quote (DDP — no customs paperwork on your end).

**Do you ship to my state?**
We ship to all 50 US states. International on request — email us.

**How do I pay?**
Credit card or ACH via Stripe at checkout. Net-30 terms available for approved B2B accounts — apply at /account/apply.

**Do you offer free shipping?**
Orders over $250 ship free in the continental US. Below that, real-time UPS/USPS rates at checkout. LA local? Pick up at the warehouse for free with code `LAPICKUP`.

**Do you sell anything besides tubes?**
Today: just tubes. Cones, mylar bags, and jars are coming in the next 6 months. Email us if you want to be notified.

**Can I get a sample?**
Yes — order a 25-pack ($9.99) and use code `WELCOME10` for 10% off. That's the fastest way to test our product.

**Are these made in the USA?**
No. They're manufactured at our partner factory in China and warehoused in LA. Made-in-USA versions are 3–4× the price; the vast majority of the cannabis industry uses China-sourced tubes for cost reasons.

**Are they recyclable?**
The plastic is #5 polypropylene, which is recyclable in most municipal programs (varies by city). We're working on a PCR (post-consumer recycled) line for 2026.

**What sizes do you stock?**
116mm (king-size / 1-gram preroll standard) is our launch SKU. 98mm and 78mm coming Q3 2026. Need a custom size? Quote form.

**Can I print my logo on the tube?**
Yes — two ways. Custom label applied to a stock tube (1K MOQ, 5–7 days, $0.18/unit blended) or full direct-to-tube print at the factory (10K MOQ, 4–6 weeks, quote-only). Quote form is at /quote.

**Are these compliant in [state]?**
Our CR test report meets US federal CR standards (16 CFR 1700) which most states adopt. We're not legal counsel — check your state's specific cannabis packaging rules. Most CA / OR / WA / NV / OK / MA / IL brands use these without issue.

**Do you offer Net-30?**
For approved B2B accounts. Apply at /account/apply with your business info and resale cert. We approve most apps in 1 business day for orders ≥ $500.

**What's your return policy?**
Unopened cases returnable within 30 days for a full refund minus return shipping. Opened cases or used product → no returns (food/medical packaging standard). Defective product replaced free with photo evidence.

**Can I see the CR test report?**
Yes — /cert. PDF is downloadable without registration.

**Are these BPA-free?**
Yes. Polypropylene #5, no BPA.

**What color is the cap?**
Matches the tube body unless noted. Black-with-silver-cap is a separate SKU.

**Can I order a mixed pack?**
Yes — the 25 / 100 / 500 retail packs come in a "Mixed" variant with equal portions of black, clear, white, and smoke.

**Do you charge sales tax?**
Yes for California buyers. B2B buyers with a resale cert on file are tax-exempt. Other states: only where we have nexus (currently CA only).

**How can I track my order?**
Tracking number emailed automatically when your order ships. Also available in your account.

**My order arrived damaged. What do I do?**
Email us at help@thepackguys.com with photos within 7 days. We'll replace or refund.

**Can I cancel an order?**
Yes if it hasn't shipped. Once shipped, it's a return.

**Who do I contact for B2B / volume questions?**
sales@thepackguys.com or use the chat widget bottom-right. Quote form at /quote for custom runs.
