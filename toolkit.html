★ Warning Copy Library ★

State-by-state, copy-paste.

Common cannabis warning text per state statute. Click into the block, select all, copy. Not legal counsel — verify against current state rules before final production.

★ California (BPC § 26120 / DCC)
GOVERNMENT WARNING: This product contains cannabis, a Schedule I controlled substance. Keep out of reach of children and animals. Cannabis products may only be possessed or consumed by persons 21 years of age or older unless the person is a qualified patient. Cannabis use while pregnant or breastfeeding may be harmful. Consumption of cannabis impairs your ability to drive and operate machinery. Please use extreme caution.
★ Oklahoma (OAC 442:10-7-1 / OMMA)
WARNING: Marijuana use while pregnant or breastfeeding may be harmful. Marijuana smoke, including secondhand smoke, has been listed as a substance known to the State of California to cause cancer and reproductive harm. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
★ Nevada (NAC 678C.620 / CCB)
WARNING: Cannabis use while pregnant or breastfeeding may be harmful. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. For use only by adults 21 years of age and older. This product contains THC, which is a controlled substance under federal law.
★ New York (NYCRR Part 124 / OCM)
WARNING: Cannabis use can be addictive and may impair concentration, coordination, and judgment. Do not operate a vehicle or machinery under its influence. Use of cannabis while pregnant or breastfeeding may be harmful. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. For use only by adults 21 and older.
★ Colorado (1 CCR 212-3 / MED)
WARNING: There may be health risks associated with consumption of this product. This product contains marijuana. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. The intoxicating effects of this product may be delayed by up to two hours. Do not drive a motor vehicle or operate heavy machinery while using marijuana.
★ Michigan (R 420.401 et seq. / CRA)
WARNING: This product contains marijuana. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. Marijuana use may impair concentration, coordination, and judgment. Do not operate a vehicle or machinery under its influence. Use of marijuana while pregnant or breastfeeding may be harmful.
★ Universal (US PPPA + safe-default)
WARNING: This product contains cannabis. For adult use only — 21+. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN AND PETS. Cannabis use may impair concentration and reaction time; do not drive or operate heavy machinery. May be habit-forming. If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, do not consume.
