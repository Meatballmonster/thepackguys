★ Color Combinations · Round 2 · 2026-05-27

Eight ENTIRELY NEW palette directions, none repeats of Round 1. New round-table voices (Kalman, Ruscha, Rams, Judd, Jongerius, Color Marketing Group). Three themes: single saturated hero, place-anchored (LA / material), 2026 emerging-supply palette.

★ Round-table 2 — what's different

Kalman: "Use ONE crazy honest color the buyer remembers in 6 months. Three colors total. No softening."
Ruscha: "Pack Guys is LA. Asphalt black, palm-shadow grey, one shocking sunset accent."
Rams: "Accent should appear in 5% of pixels, not 30%. Restraint is the differentiator."
Judd: "Own a color. UPS brown, Tiffany teal, John Deere green. What color does no cannabis B2B own? Be that."
Jongerius: "Echo the product surfaces — translucent, smoky, silver. Cool greys + silver as system, magenta as the only warm point."
Color Marketing Group: "Deep teal + saturated coral + warm bone — emerging in B2B supply for 2026."
