why we publish this

Most packaging suppliers hide prices because their pricing is negotiable on a per-buyer basis — and "negotiable" means "we'll charge more if you don't know better." Berlin Packaging, Calyx Containers, Gamut — try finding a per-unit number on any of their sites. You won't. You'll find a "request a quote" form, an account-rep call, and a delayed response.

That model works because the buyer has no anchor — no published list to argue from, no comparable per-unit number on a competitor's site. The remedy isn't memorizing supply-chain math; it's having a posted reference price you can hold any quote up against.

what changes when you stop chasing quotes

The hidden cost of an opaque packaging quote isn't only the markup — it's the calendar. A typical "request a quote" cycle eats two weeks before a single tube ships: form submission, account-rep call, NDA-flavored back-and-forth, revised quote, terms negotiation, finally a PO. For a brand prepping a launch or a re-up, that's two weeks of holding flower without packaging.

Posted tier pricing collapses that to a single click. You see the per-unit number at your case size, decide if it pencils, and order. Same-day ship from LA on anything before 1pm PT, 5 business days door-to-door anywhere in the continental US. The price discipline is a side effect — the real win is the calendar.

what's actually in the order

Every per-unit number at every tier ships with the same spec, the same cert, and the same QC standard. There is no "good / better / best" tier-gating game.

  • Spec: 116mm child-resistant pop-top, squeeze-to-open with flat cap. ASTM D3475 / 16 CFR § 1700.20 child-resistance certified — full test PDF on file, sent inside 24 hours of request.
  • Colors: five standard at launch (black, clear, white, smoke, silver). Custom-color and printed branding rolling out Q3 2026 — no MOQ change on standard SKUs while that program scales.
  • Inspection: every case is physically inspected at the LA warehouse before it ships. DOA / defect rate runs below 1% (we replace, no questions, for anything over).
  • Fulfillment SLA: orders before 1pm PT ship same business day. 5 business days door-to-door anywhere CONUS via USPS/UPS Ground. Same-day LA pickup by appointment.
  • Terms: Net-30 after your second paid invoice clears. No quote-form gating to qualify, no buyer-portal demo call.

That's the whole product — and that's where the tier price lives. No regional reps, no quarterly account reviews, no quote-form theater. The catalog page is the price list.

what you should ask your current supplier

Three questions that flip the conversation:

  1. "What's your per-unit price at 5K cases?" If they have to "circle back" — they're calling their margin manager, not their pricing system.
  2. "Do you hold inventory in California or do you order against my PO?" "Inventory" means real warehouse; "order against your PO" means they're a broker drop-shipping from China and you're waiting 8-12 weeks.
  3. "Can I see the CR test report PDF?" A real supplier sends it within 24 hours. A broker sends "we'll request it from the factory."

Your packaging spend is a leverage point — and now you have anchor pricing.
