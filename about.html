★ About · Est. 2026 ★

By Operators,

We are a wholesale preroll tube supplier in Los Angeles, run by cannabis operators who got tired of being on the wrong side of the broker conversation.

★ Chapter 01 ★

The Origin.

The team behind The Pack Guys runs an active California co-packing operation and several cannabis brands. For ten years we bought preroll tubes from brokers who marked up 30 to 40 percent, hid prices behind quote forms, and ghosted us when shipments slipped.

We finally decided to stop being the customer and become the supplier. We sourced our own factory in China, qualified the spec ourselves (CR-certified pop-top, 16 CFR 1700.20), and started stocking inventory at our LA warehouse.

Now other operators get the deal we wished we'd had: posted prices, same-day ship, Net-30 after order #2.
