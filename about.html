We run the supply room.

We're a wholesale pre-roll tube supplier in Los Angeles, run by cannabis operators who got tired of being on the wrong side of the broker conversation.

Every tube vendor we dealt with made the same boring product feel like a negotiation: "request a quote," wait two days, get a number nobody else could see, hope the spec was right when the pallet landed. So we built the opposite — the supplier with the boringly-perfect paperwork.

Posted prices on every SKU and tier. Same-day pull from an LA warehouse. And a signed Certificate of Stock in every box — lot number, spec, the initials of the person who inspected it — so your compliance team never chases paper when an audit hits.

We don't make these in the USA. Our factory does, and the CR cert is on file. We'd rather tell you that than pretend. Trust compounds when a supplier shows the work instead of claiming "premium."
