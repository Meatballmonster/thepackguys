The Pack Guys started because the brands we work with kept complaining about the same things: 6-week lead times from China, $200 sample fees from boutique brokers, CR paperwork that "didn't quite cover" the SKU that actually arrived.

We fixed it. We stock the most-ordered SKUs — black, clear, white, smoke, black-with-silver cap — in our Los Angeles warehouse. We hand-pull every CR test report ourselves and put it on the site as a downloadable PDF. We don't gatekeep prices. We don't make you "request a quote" to find out a tube is seven cents.

If you need 1,000 black tubes for your next preroll run, you can order them now and have them in 2 days. If you need 50,000 custom-printed tubes in your brand color, we'll quote you in 24 hours and ship in 4 weeks — duty included, no customs surprises at your door.

That's it. We sell tubes. We're good at it.
