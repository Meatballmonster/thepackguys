ABOUT THE PACK GUYS

By operators, for operators.

We are a wholesale pre-roll tube supplier in Los Angeles, run by cannabis operators who got tired of being on the wrong side of the broker conversation.

the origin

The team behind The Pack Guys runs an active California co-packing operation and several cannabis brands. For ten years we bought pre-roll tubes from brokers who marked up 30 to 40 percent, hid prices behind quote forms, and ghosted us when shipments slipped. We finally decided to stop being the customer and become the supplier.

Pack Guys is what we wished existed when we were the buyer. Honest pricing in public. Live inventory you can verify before you order. One LA warehouse, one twenty-four-hour ship guarantee, one phone number that rings to a human who has packed a tube before.

"Same factory as the big brands. Better LA logistics. Half the markup."

what we actually do

We import 116mm child-resistant pop-top pre-roll tubes in 40-foot container loads from a long-time factory partner, hold roughly a quarter million units in stock at our Arts District warehouse, and ship to California dispensaries and cannabis brands in five business days nationwide. Same-day pickup at 915 Mateo St for LA-county buyers.

Our 116mm tubes are certified per 16 CFR § 1700.20 and ASTM D3475, food-grade polypropylene (#5 PP), available in five standard colors. Custom-printed and custom-color runs coming Q3 2026.

what makes us different

Transparent pricing. Our catalog shows live per-unit cost at every case tier. No quote-form gatekeeping. You see the same price your neighbor sees.

Real LA inventory. When you place an order on Tuesday morning, your tubes ship Tuesday afternoon from a warehouse you can drive to. No container wait, no overseas shipment racket.

24-hour ship SLA. In-stock orders ship within one business day, or we credit your next 1K case in full. We back the promise because we hold the inventory.

Operator-grade voice. We answer email like operators because we are operators. No sales-rep call gate, no chatbots, no "circling back" with an account manager.

where we are

Our warehouse is at 915 Mateo St, Suite 312, Los Angeles, CA 90021 — in the Arts District, between 7th and 8th. By-appointment pickups welcome. Email hello@thepackguys.com to schedule.

where we are going

Phase 2 (Q3 2026): custom-color and custom-printed tubes at lower MOQs. Phase 3 (Q4 2026): king-size cones and mylar exit bags. Phase 4 (2027): bio-resin and recycled-content variants. The goal is to be the supplier you stay with as your dispensary scales, not the one you rotate away from after six months.
