One building, one crew,
one warehouse in LA.

We started because the brands we work with kept complaining about the same things: 6-week lead times from China, $200 sample fees from boutique brokers, CR paperwork that "didn't quite cover" the SKU that actually arrived.

We stock the most-ordered SKUs — black, clear, white, smoke, black-with-silver — in our LA building. We hand-pull every CR test report ourselves and put it on the site as a PDF. We don't gatekeep prices. We don't make you "request a quote" to find out a tube is 7 cents.

If you need 1,000 black tubes for Friday's pack, you can order them now and have them in 2 days. If you need 50,000 in your brand color, we quote you in 24 hours.

Every order ships with a numbered manifest, signed by the person who pulled it. That's the part we built the rest of the operation around.
