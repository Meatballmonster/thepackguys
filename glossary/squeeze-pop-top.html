Squeeze Pop-Top.

CR closure that detaches via side-squeeze

The variant of pop-top closure where the cap detaches fully when squeezed (no hinge). Pack Guys' standard closure type.

The squeeze pop-top is the specific CR closure used on Pack Guys 116mm tubes. To open: squeeze the cap at the two magenta chevron-marked points (left + right sides of the cap), then pull up. The cap detaches completely — no hinge to wear out.

Squeeze pop-tops pass 16 CFR § 1700.20 because the squeeze motion requires opposing-force fine motor coordination that children under 4 cannot reliably execute. Adults reliably can.

Compared to hinged pop-tops (cap stays attached on a plastic hinge): squeeze pop-tops have no hinge to fail over use cycles, and the detached cap allows easier label inspection at retail. They're also slightly cheaper to manufacture.
