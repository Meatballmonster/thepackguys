116mm Tube.

King-size pre-roll tube

116mm-tall pre-roll tube — the industry-standard size for king-size joints. Holds 1 cone up to 1.5g of flower.

The 116mm tube is the de-facto industry standard for king-size pre-rolls. 116mm (4.57") tall × ~16mm wide (0.63"), fits exactly one king-size cone holding up to 1.5 grams of flower.

Other common sizes: 90mm (for 0.5–1g half-gram cones), 70mm (mini), 120mm (oversized). 116mm dominates because king-size has become the default consumer format in most adult-use markets.

Pack Guys' single-spec focus is the 116mm CR squeeze pop-top in five standard colors (black, clear, white, smoke, silver). One spec done well, deeply stocked.
