Doob Tube.

Slang term for a pre-roll joint tube. Used interchangeably with "pre-roll tube,""joint tube," and "king-size tube" in cannabis B2B procurement.

"Doob tube" is the original slang term for a single-joint storage and dispensing tube, originating from the 1970s. In modern cannabis B2B procurement, it's used interchangeably with "pre-roll tube,""joint tube," and "king-size tube" depending on supplier preference.

Pack Guys uses the terms "pre-roll tube" and "116mm tube" in product copy because they're more SEO-friendly and less colloquial — but the doob tube vernacular is well understood by operators.

Whatever you call it, the spec is the same: a child-resistant container sized to hold one joint, typically 116mm tall for king-size formats.
