★ Where #1 wins

  • Visual impact — at hero scale, Bebas Neue is loud + confident; reads as magazine-cover masthead.
  • Wider audience appeal — buyers who aren't operators (designers, agency teams) read all-caps masthead as "premium brand."
  • Most used by adjacent brands — Patagonia, Casper, Allbirds use Bebas-family fonts. Familiar territory.

Risk: common font (free + popular) — relies on the cobalt+magenta system + the rest of your brutalist context to stay ownable.

★ Where #2 wins

  • Already loaded — JetBrains Mono is on every page; zero new font-loading cost.
  • Most on-brand voice — operator-built, no-marketing-fluff. Underscores between words read as "package name / SKU."
  • Distinctive in B2B packaging — no other tube supplier uses a mono wordmark. Standing out is the win.

Risk: reads as "tech / dev tool" to some buyers; could feel cold to brand-designer audiences. Operators love it; agencies might not.
