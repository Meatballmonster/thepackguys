What we don't do

  • We don't custom-print. Not at launch. We're keeping it to standard SKUs while we prove the operation. If you need custom-printed tubes, the China factory we source from can do it directly; ask and we'll connect you.
  • We don't pre-fill tubes. Empty tubes only. The moment we pre-filled one with cannabis "as a sample" we'd need a cannabis license, and that's not the business we're in.
  • We don't run drip campaigns. If you give us your email for a sample kit, you get exactly one email when the kit arrives, plus a tracking update. No newsletter, no nurture sequence. If you want to talk, email us.
  • We don't hide prices behind quote forms. The whole point of this catalog is that you can see what you'd pay before you give us your phone number.

What every order ships with

Every box we ship from 915 Mateo St includes a 4×6 kraft cardstock manifest. On it: a sequential manifest number, the SKU and quantities, the initials of the person who pulled the order, the pull date and time, the weather in LA that day, and a one-line dispatch. There's a small Customs Orange stamp and a QR code in the bottom-right linking to the CR cert PDF and your tracking page.

It's tucked face-up on top of the contents. Drop it in your compliance binder, photograph it, throw it out - it does what you need it to do.
