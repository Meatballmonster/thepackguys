questions, answered.

are these certified child-resistant?
Yes. Every tube meets 16 CFR § 1700.20 and ASTM D3475 standards. CR test reports on file from our manufacturer — we send the PDF on request.
what's the minimum order?
A 1,000-unit case is the standard MOQ. For first-time buyers, our $14.99 100-unit trial case is the lowest-friction way in. No commitment.
do you offer net-30 terms?
Yes — after your second order. First order is upfront (card or wire); approved wholesale accounts get net-30 thereafter.
how fast do orders ship?
In-stock SKUs ship within 24 hours from our LA warehouse. Most orders arrive in 3–5 business days within the continental US. Same-day pickup available at 915 Mateo St for LA-county buyers.
can i get a custom color or printed tube?
Custom runs are coming Q3 2026. Standard MOQ for custom color: 25,000 units, ~4-week lead time direct from factory. Drop your email on a "Coming" tile in our catalog to be notified.
are these tubes recyclable?
Our 116mm CR tubes are polypropylene (#5 plastic), recyclable in municipal programs that accept #5. Bio-resin and recycled-content options are on our roadmap for late 2026.
where are you based?
Los Angeles. Our warehouse is at 915 Mateo St in the Arts District. Tubes are manufactured by our long-time factory partner and shipped to LA in 40-ft container loads — we hold ~250K units in stock so you can order 1K and receive in 5 days. We're a California company supplying California cannabis (and beyond).
what's your return policy?
Unopened cases returnable within 30 days for a full refund minus shipping. Defective product replaced free of charge. Custom orders are non-returnable once production starts.
