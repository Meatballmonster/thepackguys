Carriers

We ship via UPS Ground, FedEx Ground, and USPS Priority — whichever rate is lowest for your zone and weight. Want a specific carrier? Note it on your order and we'll match where possible.

Rates

Shipping rates are carrier-calculated and passed through at cost. We don't mark up shipping. Live rates appear on your invoice after we pack and weigh the actual cartons.

Typical ranges for a 1,000-unit case (single carton, ~12 lbs):

  • West Coast (CA, NV, AZ, OR, WA): $18–$32
  • Mountain / Plains: $28–$48
  • Midwest / South: $35–$60
  • Northeast / East Coast: $42–$72

Multi-case orders consolidate to pallet rates above ~10,000 units. We'll quote LTL freight on orders 25,000+.

Zones & Transit Times

From LA (zip 90021), standard ground transit:

  • CA, NV, AZ, UT: 1–2 business days
  • OR, WA, ID, CO, NM, TX: 2–3 business days
  • Midwest: 3–4 business days
  • East Coast: 4–5 business days

These are after the 5-business-day processing window. Need it faster? Email sales@thepackguys.com for expedited quotes.

LA County Same-Day Pickup

Will-call pickup available at 915 Mateo St, Suite 312, Los Angeles, CA 90021. Email 24-hours ahead with your time window and order #. We'll have it palletized and ready at the loading dock.

International Shipping

Currently US-only on the website. International quotes available by request — we can ship containers ex-factory from China direct to your warehouse for orders 100K+. Email for a custom quote.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your shipment arrives damaged, photograph the outer carton + contents before unpacking further and email help@thepackguys.com within 48 hours. We'll file the carrier claim and either credit your account or reship — your choice.

Lost shipments (tracking shows delivered but you don't have it): we'll wait 48 hours for stragglers, then file the carrier claim and reship.
