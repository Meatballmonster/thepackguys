same-day LA pickup.

LA-county buyers can skip carriers entirely. Email hello@thepackguys.com at least 24 hours ahead to schedule. Confirmed orders placed before noon PT are ready for pickup at 915 Mateo St, Suite 312 the same business day. By appointment only — we are not open to walk-ups.

lead times explained.

Our quoted "5 business days door-to-door" is the median for in-stock SKUs to continental US destinations. The breakdown:

  • Day 0: Order placed (before noon PT cutoff)
  • Day 1: Picked, packed, labels printed, handed to carrier from 915 Mateo
  • Days 2-4: In carrier transit (USPS / UPS / FedEx / LTL)
  • Day 5: Delivered to your warehouse

West Coast destinations typically arrive in 2-3 days (USPS Priority). East Coast destinations may extend to 4-5 days. Outside continental US (Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, territories): add 2-4 days. We do not currently ship internationally.

If we miss the 24-hour ship window on an in-stock SKU, your next 1K case ships free as a credit. See SLA terms.

tracking + handoff.

You will receive a tracking email the moment your shipment leaves 915 Mateo, with:

  • Carrier name (USPS / UPS / FedEx / LTL broker)
  • Tracking number + direct carrier link
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Expected box count and total weight
  • SLA reminder if 24-hour window was met

Title and risk of loss pass to you at carrier handoff. If a shipment is lost, delayed beyond 7 days, or arrives damaged, email help@thepackguys.com within 7 days of expected delivery and we will work directly with the carrier on the claim. See full terms.

address requirements + delivery.

To avoid delays:

  • Use the registered business address — residential delivery for wholesale quantities often gets rerouted to commercial holds at the carrier
  • Provide a phone number the carrier can call if there is an issue at delivery (LTL pallet shipments require this)
  • Confirm receiving hours — most carriers deliver 9am-5pm local; outside those hours, LTL freight needs scheduling
  • Specify dock vs street delivery for pallet quantities — dock is free, street-level (liftgate) is $75

damaged or missing orders.

If your shipment arrives damaged or missing items:

  • Take photos before opening if the box looks compromised on arrival
  • Refuse the shipment at delivery if damage is severe (especially LTL pallets) — the carrier records the refusal
  • Email us within 7 days of delivery date with order number, photos, and a short description of the damage at help@thepackguys.com
  • We replace defective tubes free of charge, including return shipping if needed
