where CoolJarz is the right answer

Two scenarios where CoolJarz is genuinely the better pick:

  1. Your brand requires "Made in USA" packaging. Some CA dispensary partners and some retail-buyer programs have written-in requirements about domestic manufacturing. CoolJarz tubes are made at their Tustin, CA facility. Pack Guys tubes are imported, then LA-stocked. If the requirement is real (and not just preference), pick CoolJarz.
  2. You need to consolidate jars + tubes + vials with one supplier. CoolJarz has a broader packaging line. If you're buying jars and bags too, the consolidation savings can outweigh per-line price differences.

where Pack Guys is the right answer

Four scenarios:

  1. You hate quote forms. Every Pack Guys per-unit number is on the catalog page. Tier 1 ($0.085 at 1K cases) through Tier 4 ($0.060 at 50K). No form, no email gate, no account-rep call.
  2. You want Net-30 without filling out a credit application. Pack Guys extends Net-30 after your second paid invoice clears. No separate app, no trade references demanded, no buyer-portal demo call.
  3. You want to sample before committing. $14.99 trial case (100 units, any standard color). The full $14.99 is credited to your first wholesale order. Order a sample in two minutes.
  4. You pay in crypto. Pack Guys accepts USDC, BTC, and ETH with a 1.5% prompt-pay discount on USDC.
